About DUAD To-Do List

DUAD To-Do List is a task management application designed to help users organize their day-to-day life efficiently. Built with vanilla JavaScript, this tool offers an intuitive and functional interface without relying on external frameworks, ensuring optimal performance and a smooth experience.

Key Features:

  • Simple and minimalistic: Easy-to-use interface for task management.
  • Quick management: Create, edit, and delete tasks with just a few clicks.
  • Progress tracking: Mark completed tasks for better organization.
  • Flexible organization: Categorize and prioritize tasks as needed.

DUAD To-Do List is the result of my passion for development and efficiency in managing daily activities. My goal with this application is to provide a lightweight, practical, and accessible solution for any user who needs to better structure their day. I hope you enjoy using DUAD To-Do List as much as I enjoyed creating it!
